Google AdSense Kaise Use Karein

1

Google AdSense Account Banayein

Sabse pehle Google AdSense website par jakar account create karein. Aapke paas valid Gmail ID aur website honi chahiye.

2

Website Ko Verify Karein

Google AdSense aapki website ko verify karega. Is process mein 1-2 hafta lag sakta hai. Aapko ek code milega jise aapko apni website mein add karna hoga.

3

Ad Units Banayein

Verification ke baad, aap apne AdSense dashboard se different types ke ads create kar sakte hain:

  • Display Ads: Website ke sidebar ya content ke beech mein
  • In-Feed Ads: Content feed ke beech mein
  • In-Article Ads: Articles ke beech mein
  • Matched Content: Related content suggestions with ads
4

Ad Code Ko Website Mein Add Karein

Google AdSense se generated code ko apni website mein jahan ads dikhane hain, wahan add karein. ShortsMaker Pro mein multiple ad slots already configured hain.

5

Payment Setup Karein

Apna bank account ya payment details AdSense mein add karein. ₹5,000 (approx) ke threshold ko cross karne ke baad, Google har mahine payment release karega.

Google AdSense Se Zyada Earning Ke Tips

  • Quality Content Create Karein: High-quality, unique content se engaged visitors milenge
  • Traffic Badhayen: Social media, SEO, aur email marketing se traffic increase karein
  • Ad Placement Optimize Karein: Ads ko strategic locations par place karein jahan sabse zyada visibility mile
  • Mobile Optimization: Apni website ko mobile-friendly banayein kyunki zyada traffic mobile se aata hai
  • Page Load Speed: Website ko fast loading banayein jisse visitors engage rahe
  • Auto Ads Try Karein: Google ke AI-powered auto ads apne aap optimal placement choose karte hain
  • Ad Balance: Bahut zyada ads na lagayein - user experience maintain karein

AdSense Earnings Calculate Kaise Hoti Hai?

Google AdSense revenue primarily depends on:

Impressions

Kitni baar ad dikha hai

CTR (Click-Through Rate)

Kitne % visitors ne ad par click kiya

CPC (Cost Per Click)

Har click ke liye kitna payment milta hai

RPM (Revenue Per Mille)

1000 impressions par kitna revenue

Basic Formula:

Earnings = Impressions × CTR × CPC

Example: 10,000 impressions × 1% CTR × ₹10 CPC = ₹1,000
